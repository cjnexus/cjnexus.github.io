About Us

NEXUS is a close-knit community made up of many CJCians passionate about investment and entrepreneurship – many of whom are curious amateurs taking their first steps to explore investment and entrepreneurship.

In learning how to manage and grow money as investors and entrepreneurs, we share what we’ve learned with the greater CJ and JC community through college-wide events and national/ regional competitions.

While many Nexians joined without any prior experience, all of us have leveraged the workshops and speaker sessions to cut our teeth in numerous external competitions. We are proud of our peers who have gone on and secured national & regional placings allowing us to become one of the most awarded CCAs in CJC.

Despite our Nexian tasks, we are still able to put sufficient time on academics – securing one of the best academic performances among CCAs.

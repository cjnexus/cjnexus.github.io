Achievements

Second Place Of BizComp 2022

CJC Nexus BizComp is our annual flagship competition that invites participants to pitch their business ideas, competing against teams from various JCs and putting their entrepreneurial skills and resilience to test.

Our Team NATYV, won second place out of 9 teams with "HOMEMBER", a smartwatch aimed to assist people with dementia not only find their way home but provide other smart services like reminders and emergency calling.

Top 4 Of Young Entrepreneur's Challenge 2022

ACS(I) organises the Young Entrepreneurs' Challenge annually, inviting JCs and Polytechnics to participate. This year's theme, "Lean Operations for Cost Reduction," prompted teams to implement cost-efficient strategies for their business ideas. The competition required teams to deliver an elevator pitch to a panel of angel investors and industry professionals.

In our first live presentation since the pandemic, Team Money Trees came in fourth place among over 30 teams with “Grow Your Wealth” which offers growing plants that can produce organic food easily to busy millennials!

Champion Of 10th Micron NUS-ISE Business Analytics Case Competition

Micron NUS-ISE Business Analytics Case Competition is a competition where participants were exposed to real world industry challenges and learned more about industry practices and system engineering to solve these challenges.

In 2021, our Nexus members were the Champions of the Pre-University Category. They researched and analyzed the semiconductor industry to give recommendations to the given circumstance!

National Winner Of 2021 FedEx Express/JA International Trade Challenge

The FedEx Express/JA International Trade Challenge, organized by JA Asia Pacific and sponsored by FedEx Express, aims to foster students' business skills and entrepreneurial mindset. Participants craft market entry strategies for a specific product and target audience.

After rigorous preparation and product refinement, CJC's Team Money Trees won the competition with Zenith Cargo. Our triumph allowed us to represent Singapore in the Asia-Pacific stage.

First Place Winner Of Youth Financial Analysis Competition 2021

SJI International organized the Youth Financial Analysis Competition 2021, welcoming high schoolers aged 13-18 globally. Contestants hailed from countries like the USA, Japan, the United Arab Emirates, and Vietnam.

Our Nexus team claimed the top spot among 300+ teams worldwide in a rigorous buy call presentation. We presented our Intel investment case to judges from Tower Capital Asia.

First Runner-Up Of Citi Foundation-SMU FinLit Challenge 2021

The Citi Foundation-SMU Financial Literacy Challenge for Young Adults is a yearly competition hosted by the Citi Foundation and SMU Financial Literacy Program. Its mission is to equip young individuals with financial literacy skills that will prepare them for future financial challenges.

In an exciting outer-space-themed challenge that covered financial management basics, Team Money Trees competed against the top 8 finalists. Despite the time constraints, our team utilized our knowledge of personal finance to complete the task as quickly as possible, earning us a well-deserved second place.

Second Place Of Youth Entrepreneur's Challenge 2021

ACS(I) annually hosts the Young Entrepreneurs' Challenge for JCs and Polytechnics. This year's theme was "Lean Operations for Cost Reduction," where teams devised cost-efficient strategies for their business ideas. Contestants delivered an elevator pitch to impress angel investors and other professionals.

Team Lexicon secured second place with their brilliant idea: an intuitive note-sharing platform and a subscription-based network of online tutors to help O-Level students. The angel investors were impressed, and one even expressed interest in developing the concept further. We are incredibly proud of Team Lexicon's success!

Top 6 Of Raffles Business Symposium 2021

The Raffles Business Symposium fosters student entrepreneurship by offering a platform to present innovative ideas and receive industry expert feedback.

Our team, Hectnif, presented Financent, a digital integrated system to streamline accounting, consulting, and networking services for hawker businesses during crises. Our creative approach earned us a place in the final round, finishing in the top six teams.

Events

GIC Personal Finance Workshop

GIC is one of the three major investment entities in Singapore that manage the Government’s reserves alongside with the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) and Temasek. The organisation has also made its mission to promote financial literacy among Singaporean youths and we are fortunate to have them over introducing the matter to our members.

In this workshop, our members were exposed to the importance of personal finance management and learnt the steps to take the first control over their personal finance.

Reactor Entrepreneurship Workshops

This series of workshops is focused on nurturing entrepreneurial skills among Nexians. In the span of 8 sessions, our members have grasped the skills and knowledge required to formulate their own startup pitch.

Aside from learning in theory, our members took the chance to apply their newly-acquired skills through collaborating with other participants for a demo pitching session at the end of the workshop. As ideas clashed and sparked from hectic discussions, participants had their first taste of working towards a startup albeit in a mock-up form.

Nexus Financial Analyst Competition

Stonks or stomp? That’s the question. Highly anticipated by our Nexus members, Nexus has spearheaded finance workshops, organised and prepared by our very own members. Members learn to evaluate companies based on an array of indicators to justify them warranting a profitable investment.

Similarly, our members are to analyse and evaluate a company of their choice and share their findings to the rest of Nexus at the final session. We have invited members of NUS Investment Society to judge which team is the best!

Bizweek - 2021

Bizweek 2021 aimed to introduce the world of business to the fresh J1 students who had just entered JC. The event comprised of various activities ranging from speaker sessions both by external and internal parties to mini business games to pitching sessions by teams from various schools.

Bizweek also served as an introduction of Nexus Society to the J1s as they gained insights on Nexus’ activities and CCA routine.

Into The Cryptoverse: VR Experience

As part of Nexus’s goals to promote financial literacy, Nexus had utilised VR technology to educate both CJ staff and students on the issue of cryptocurrency. Participants entered a virtual gallery in the VR world and are free to explore the various artefacts with the help of our tour guides. Each artefact provided insights and information regarding cryptocurrency to the participants.

The event was a hit in the school with over 135 participants in a week and a total play time of more than 2,000 minutes. Participants had shown interest in the works of Nexus, translating into higher demand for more school-wide Nexus events.

EntreConnect

EntreConnect is 2-day event jointly initiated by 5 Investment and Entrepreneurship Clubs from 5 different JCs, supported by SGExams and Potato Production. The event aims to empower students by making them aware of career paths through sharings from various seasoned entrepreneurs.
